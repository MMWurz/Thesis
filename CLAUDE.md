## Title:
Challenging the fusion era: How a potential lithium-6 bottleneck is jeopardizing Europes transition into the fusion age.

## Topic:
A multi-objective optimization (MOO) of lithium-6 supply chains to provide tritium for fusion. 

## Research Goal
Develop a bi-criterion optimization model for lithium-6 supply chains optimizing:
- Cost
- EU supply risk

Environmental impact is excluded as a third objective: a rigorous ecological assessment requires LCA inventory data per process step, which does not yet exist for the novel Li-6 enrichment processes under consideration. Adding a proxy without proper data would undermine scientific validity. The exclusion is documented as a limitation and direction for future work.

Enrichment technology cost is treated as a scenario variable (low/medium/high per technology) rather than a fixed input, since public techno-economic data for novel enrichment routes is sparse. The model is run per scenario; results show how the Pareto front shifts under different cost assumptions.

## Methodology
- Estimate Li-6 requirement per GW fusion power
- Model supply chain pathways for Li-6 production and sourcing visualize as Flowchart
- Apply multi-objective optimization to evaluate trade-offs
